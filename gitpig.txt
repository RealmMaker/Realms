So, this is after I have commited.
Science taught me, and too often I see life imitate this art:

Nothing new emerges from sameness.
In sameness there is preservation, cohesiveness, comfort, identity even, but never any realisation beyond what is to the limitless possibilities of what could be.

A man remains a man. Likewise a woman, a woman; but a baby, alongside all its combined potential is only born when the two come together.
